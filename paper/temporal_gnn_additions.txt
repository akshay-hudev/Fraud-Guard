\subsection{Temporal Graph Neural Networks}
Static GNNs treat the fraud network as fixed, but real provider
rings evolve over time. Temporal GNNs address this by modelling
time-stamped interactions. Xu et al.~\cite{xu2020tgat} propose
TGAT using time-encoding and attention over temporal neighbours.
Rossi et al.~\cite{rossi2020tgn} introduce TGN with a memory
module that maintains evolving node states. Trivedi et
al.~\cite{trivedi2019dyrep} model event-driven structural changes.
These approaches are directly relevant to detecting ring fraud
that emerges gradually across billing periods.
